PJ's Newsletter

Who am I?

My name is PJ Kim.

I'm 18 years old, and I love to make things. I currently attend Wayne State University majoring in electrical and computer engineering.

My interests include math, coding, engineering, film, and photography. My dream is to build things to inspire and help people. My current passion project is my Youtube Channel where I aim to combine film and engineering to share my ideas.

What is my newsletter?

Synopsis: a collection of thoughts, ideas, and learnings.

The goal of this newsletter is to connect with people. Through sharing my thoughts, I aim to provoke inquiry amongst subscribers. On the more personal side, this newsletter acts as a log to record my experiences. I post stories about anything that interests me.

Why subcribe?

Subscribe to recieve emails about updates and newsletters.

Subscribing acts as a way of keeping in touch with me and providing an opportunity to share your opinion through email. If you have any questions, comments, concerns, or just want to reach out, you can respond to the email's sent via my newsletter.

Lets keep in touch!
